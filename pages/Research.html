Similarities and differences between the nav bar of FDIUb Tech and w3schools

FDIUb Tech
w3schools

Similarities

In both sites FDIUb Tech and w3schools the nav bar is postioned under the header of the site. Nav bar of both the sites have an hover effect while hover over the contents of the nav bar. While hovering over the contents of nav bar of both sites the background of the content changes.

Differences

FDIUb Tech

FDIUb Tech
The nav bar doesn't use accordion. The nav bar use accordion.
The color of text in nav bar doesn't change when hovering over it. The color of text in nav bar changes when hovering over it.
No presence of search bar in nav bar. Presence of a search bar in nav bar.
Nav bar has a background color of blue. Nav bar has a background color of green.
The contents of nav bar are centrally aligned. The contents of nav bar are aligned on the left side.
Nav bar doesnt have javascript. Nav bar has a javascript that allows the users to change the language of the entire site.

Similarities and differences between the blog page of FDIUb Tech and Blogging Basics 101

FDIUb Tech
Blogging Basics 101

Differences

FDIUb Tech

Blogging Basics 101

No persence of comment section in after the blog. Persence of comment section in after the blog.
Text align justified in the blog. Text align left in the blog.
No image in main is displayed inline. Some images in main is displayed inline.
Background color of wrapper class and main different. Background color of wrapper class and main same.
Background color of blog blue and text color white. Background color of blog white and text color black.

FDIUb Tech
Islington college

Similarities

Differences

FDIUb Tech

Islington College
There is no captcha in the form. There is a captcha in the form.
There are two input fields in the form. There are three input fields in the form.

The hidden div that appears if the input fields are empty uses style visibility:hidden The hidden div that appears if the input fields are empty uses style display:none
